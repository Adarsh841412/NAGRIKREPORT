About Nagrik Report

Nagrik Report is a smart digital governance platform designed to bridge the gap between citizens and government authorities. It allows users to report real-life issues such as road damage, garbage problems, water leakage, and electricity failures with ease.

The system ensures that each complaint is automatically routed to the correct department, enabling faster resolution and accountability. Citizens can track their complaints in real-time, while officers can efficiently manage and resolve issues through a dedicated dashboard.

Our mission is to create a transparent, efficient, and citizen-friendly system for better public service delivery 🚀
